Classes

Classes at Flagler Drums need to be scheduled through a direct conversation with one of our teachers - Jay, Greyson, or Neal. Please call our email us to schedule a FREE consultation with the prospective student so we can assess their current level, and set them up with a teaching schedule that will work financially and time-wise. Prices are not set as they are dictated by the student's needs, but they are in the range of 20 - 40 dollars an hour.

Call (386) 233-2034 or email us at classes@flaglerdrums.com to schedule a consultation!
