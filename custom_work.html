Flager Drums Custom Work

Since 2010, Flagler Drums has partnered with a local drum builder, Joe Siberiano, to create custom drum kits of any shape and size. If you can dream it up, we can make it happen! All price points are determined by the work, so if you are interested in a custom kit, please reach out to us by email at customs@flaglerdrums.com.
