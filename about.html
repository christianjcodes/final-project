About Flagler Drums

Founded in 2005 by local drummer Jay Weinberg, Flagler Drums has served the surrounding Flagler/Palm Coast area by providing drum sets, gear, classes, as well as contributing to and supporting local music festivals. As a local drummer, Jay has a heart for his community and wants to spread his love of drums to others.

After a few years, Jay partnered with two other drummers - Greyson Nekrutman and Neal Peart, two other locally known drummers. Together, these three musicians have brought Flagler Drums to where it is today, and hope to continue to do so for the foreseeable future.
