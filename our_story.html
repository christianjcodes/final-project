Our Story

Jay Weinberg was born in 1964, right here in Flagler Beach, FL. He spent most of his growing up living the Flagler life - back then, there was much less infrastructure than there is now, so it was even quieter and simpler. From before he could walk, he demonstrated rhythmic capabilities, always clapping or tapping on random objects. As a toddler, he took this further by taking wooden spoons with him anywhere in the house, finding various furniture to mark up.

So, at age 3, his parents bought him a child-size drum set to direct all this energy into something productive. He was hooked right away. He played constantly all growing up, mostly self-taught with some instruction here and there from local drummers.

As a teenager, he played in local bands constantly, with the dream of being a famous drummer one day. And when he was about to enter his twenties, he did indeed tour with a few notable bands as a touring drummer, an experience that lasted about ten years off and on. In between tours he was a local instructor and drum renovator - buying used kits and fixing them up to perfection, then reselling for profit. In this time, he also learned how to craft custom drums from local drum builder Joe Siberiano.

After that decade was coming to a close, though, Jay grew tired of the touring lifestyle. It wasn't all it was cracked up to be, and involved a lot more headaches than he thought it was worth. He'd always kept Flagler as his home base, and realized that he'd much rather just be there most of the year than out on the road. Plus, he'd always be able to play drums no matter the setting or location.

So, in 2005, after about a year of business research and finding a suitable spot, Jay opened up Flagler Drums right here in Flagler Beach. As Jay was already locally known and famous, it took off right away. Jay had a full teaching schedule in the first week of opening. And, he has maintained that almost constantly till this day. Jay loves this shop and community, and truly believes that this is what he was meant to do with his drumming skills.

Jay now runs the shop with two of his close drummer friends, Greyson Nekrutman and Neal Peart, and loves every minute of it. Come stop by and and join our community, we'd love to have you!
